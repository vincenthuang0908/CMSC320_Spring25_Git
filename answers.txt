Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: It has version control so you have a history of different versions of your code. 
Also, you can use it to collaborate with others where different people can push code onto a repository on GitHub.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Finance, for instance you could analyze stock market data using data science to make predictive models 
to predict what direction the market is going to go.
